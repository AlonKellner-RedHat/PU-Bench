## Variance Decomposition

**Demonstrates tight error margins when stratified by dataset**

**Example: VPU-nomix-MP(0.5), AUC**

| Dataset | Mean | Std (seed variance) | n |
|---------|------|---------------------|---|
| 20News | 0.5719 | **0.1530** | 75 |
| Connect4 | 0.8003 | **0.1107** | 75 |
| FashionMNIST | 0.9914 | **0.0088** | 75 |
| IMDB | 0.7974 | **0.0799** | 75 |
| MNIST | 0.9735 | **0.0364** | 75 |
| Mushrooms | 0.9725 | **0.0569** | 75 |
| Spambase | 0.8136 | **0.1718** | 75 |

**Summary:**
- Grand mean (across all datasets): 0.8458
- Average within-dataset std (seed variance): **0.0882**
- Cross-dataset std (dataset heterogeneity): 0.1495

**Variance ratio**: Dataset heterogeneity is **1.7× larger** than seed variance

**Key insight**: By stratifying, we get **1.7× tighter error margins** (0.088 vs 0.149)
